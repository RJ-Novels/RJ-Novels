“If you keep my secret account hidden, I’ll do something good for you, okay?”

Aisaka Akito happened to discover that Mamiya Yuu, the model honor student who sat next to him, was actually a “secret account girl.” To keep his mouth shut, she made him tag along as her photographer for those hidden account photos.
