Description

“You’ll never have to work for the rest of your life, okay?”

A heart-pounding cohabitation begins—with a beautiful, wealthy, and slightly mysterious older woman!

After my dad suddenly disappeared, I lost both my home and my part-time job.

The one who came to my rescue was a regular customer from the café where I worked—an elegant older woman.

She offered to let me stay at her place on one condition: that I help her out with household chores until I can get back on my feet.

But… she handed me a wad of cash just for tidying up!? Who is this woman!?
