Q: What was your motivation for applying to this high school?

A: Because my wife was going to go to school here.

I, who was already in my 30s, suddenly woke up and found myself back 15 years ago.

In front of me was my wife, Yuki, wearing a school uniform that I didn’t recognize.

Since we were supposed to meet only after becoming adults, we were still strangers at the moment

•

—But I knew everything.

I knew that at this time, she didn’t trust anyone, had no friends, and felt isolated.

I know how much she regretted those gray teenage years after becoming an adult.

Now, I’m going to change everything.

“Why do you know my personal secrets!?”

“Of course, because I’m your (future) husband.”

This is my story, of how I recolored my wife’s gray teenage years with my own colors.
