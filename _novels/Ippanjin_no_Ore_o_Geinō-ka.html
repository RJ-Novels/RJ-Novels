Description

Elite Girls Can’t Leave This Guy Alone!!

“I’m just in the academic track…?”

Yuto Aoi, the secret son of a nationally beloved actor, enrolls at Ichiyo High School, a prestigious academy with diverse programs. All he wants is a quiet, ordinary student life in the academic track, but the girls in the performing arts program won’t leave him alone!

His childhood friend Akari Hotta, an aspiring idol, is so close to him that people suspect they’re dating. Meanwhile, he ends up living next door to Minase Nagisa, a rising young actress his age, leading to a lively and attention-grabbing daily life! To top it off, Yuto inherited the striking looks of his father, once hailed as a “national treasure-level heartthrob,” making him a hidden hottie with untapped acting talent.

When Nagisa recruits him as her partner for the performing arts exam, Yuto finds himself surrounded by charming, talented girls in this dazzling performing arts academy rom-com!
