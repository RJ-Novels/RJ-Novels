”It’s just not right for a childhood friend who has loved the protagonist for over ten years to lose to some transfer student who just showed up out of nowhere!”

Himemiya Manaka is the childhood friend who has spent the better part of her life with the protagonist, Kanata. However, one day, a beautiful black-haired transfer student who looks exactly like a “Main Heroine” starts making moves on him—!?

Manaka can’t afford to play fair anymore.

To an outsider, they look like a devoted married couple, but despite her best efforts to maintain that image, they aren’t even actually dating. They haven’t even done… that yet. Meanwhile, the transfer student has already had a “fated encounter” with Kanata and is openly looking for a boyfriend.

At this rate, the childhood friend (Manaka) will naturally lose her place as a Losing Heroine. If that’s the case—

”You aren’t allowed to do these things with anyone but me, okay?”

Under the guise of “practice for a relationship,” she begins building up a history of “established facts” between them!

A rom-com brought to you by a self-proclaimed Losing Heroine whose victory is already guaranteed!
