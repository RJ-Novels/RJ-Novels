Description

Looking anxious, you ask, “Are you sure… about me?”

And that’s exactly why I want to stay by your side.

Fukusuke, a nice guy but too plain for his own good, falls in love at first sight with the most beautiful girl in class—Yua.

But the one rumored to be with Yua isn’t him—it’s his best friend, Yūsei, the ace of the basketball team.

Fukusuke sighs, knowing his feelings are hopeless.

Then one day, he notices her—the girl hidden in Yua’s shadow, unnoticed by most.

A super cute, yet completely overlooked girl—Amari Usuba.

“U-um… would you… be my friend?”

Shy and reserved, she asks hesitantly.

And so, the two of them begin practicing how to be “normal” together.

But—

“Amari-chan… the person you admire is Yūsei, isn’t it?”

“……”

“Wait, why is your face turning so red?”

A romantic comedy about a hidden beauty only I know—starting from friendship.
