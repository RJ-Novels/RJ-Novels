Description

Yatsuhashi Hazuki is just a regular high school student at Kuroba High and also the student council president.

But lately, because the number of students has been going down, Kuroba High is being merged with an all-girls school—Shiramine Girls’ High.

Since this is the first year of the merger, both schools will share the student council, led by the presidents from each school.

The president from Shiramine is a beautiful, cool, and almost perfect girl named Yukimiya Hyouka—often called the “Ice Goddess.”
