Description

Just Days of Descent

Yuito Enoki, a high school boy with no place to belong, has a woman he admires. Rui Hazuki, a college student who always buys just a single pack of cigarettes at the convenience store where he works part-time. One day, when he discovers she lives in the apartment next door, the casual moments they share on the balcony begin to bring them closer. Skipping school to hang out at a café, spending a night with her when she’s drunk—while Yuito is swept along by Rui’s free-spirited nature, he finds himself growing increasingly dependent on her. Drowning in their decadent relationship with the older, beautiful smoker, where will this life lead them?
