Description

**”A Sweet and Lovey-Dovey Romantic Comedy with the Elegant and Refined Princess [Luna] —A Half-Cohabitation Life Begins After Saving Her?”**

Seito Kiriyama, an ordinary high school student, finds himself in the middle of a kidnapping during summer vacation.

He rescues a girl named [Luna] , who was about to be taken away, and ends up living under the same roof with her because she has nowhere else to go.

Luna, who has a beautiful appearance, a stunning figure, and a cheerful personality, gradually draws Seito’s attention as they live together.

Then one day, on the first day of the new school term, Luna suddenly appears as a transfer student in Seito’s class.

It turns out that Luna is actually a princess from a certain country—and she likes Seito so much that she even proposes to him!?

“W-Well, I only do this kind of thing for you, Seito-sama… I wouldn’t do it for anyone else…”

A sweet and heartwarming high school romantic comedy with the elegant and refined princess [Luna]
