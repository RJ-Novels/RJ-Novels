Description

“It’s been a while, hasn’t it?! So why haven’t you noticed me?!”

The heartwarming yet maddening age-gap romantic comedy between a former annoying brat turned stunning high school girl begins!

After quitting a toxic job in Tokyo, 28-year-old Yuu Arigetsu returns to his hometown, eager to start a new life. But right off the bat, he crosses paths with a mysterious, pure-hearted high school beauty. She challenges him to a game: “Guess my name!” But how could he possibly know the name of a girl he’s just met? Just who *is* she…?
